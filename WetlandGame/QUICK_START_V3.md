## 🎮 How to Play - 7 NEW Wetland Levels!

### Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click "Start Game"
3. Enter your player name
4. Click "Begin First Level"

### 🔊 IMPORTANT: AUDIO IS NOW LOUD!

## 🌍 The 7 Wetland Levels

### Level 1: 💧 Freshwater Marsh
**What's Happening**: Turtles need to nest, but plastic trash covers the marsh!

**Your 3 Missions**:
1. 🗑️ Remove floating plastic pollution
2. 🐢 Protect turtle nesting grounds
3. 🌾 Restore native marsh vegetation

**Key Choices**:
- ✅ Clean up trash → Helps turtles AND reduces pollution
- ✅ Plant vegetation → Improves water filtering and fish habitat
- ❌ Ignore it → Makes everything worse!

**Audio**: Deep, grounding tone (110Hz - A2)

---

## 📊 Winning Strategy

To complete EACH level, you need:
- ✅ All 3 missions completed
- ✅ Biome health ≥ 70%
- ✅ Pollution ≤ 30%

**Button Status**:
- 🟡 Yellow/Gray = "⚠️ Complete Missions & Clean Biome" (Not ready)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "I don't hear the music!"
- ✅ Check volume slider on your device
- ✅ Unmute the game 🔊 button
- ✅ Try different browser (Chrome/Firefox work best)
- ✅ Refresh the page

### "Music is too loud!"
- ✅ Click 🔊 to mute
- ✅ Lower device volume
- ✅ Open volume options on 🔊 button

### "I can't complete the level!"
- ✅ Make sure ALL 3 missions are done
- ✅ Get health to 70% or higher
- ✅ Get pollution to 30% or lower
- ✅ Check the progress message below choices

### "Game won't load"
- ✅ Make sure all files are in same folder
- ✅ Try opening index.html directly
- ✅ Clear browser cache and refresh
