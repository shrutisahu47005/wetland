# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - Mira's Wetland Game

## How to Play

### 1. **Open the Game**
- Double-click `index.html` or open in your web browser
- The beautiful start screen loads automatically

### 2. **Start Screen (10-15 seconds)**
You'll see:
- 🌿 A serene wetland scene with animated clouds, reeds, and water ripples
- 🐦 Birds flying across the sky
- 📱 A welcome message box with the game title and description
- A prominent green **"Start Game"** button with a play icon

### 3. **Click "Start Game"**
Experience:
- Smooth fade-out of the start screen (1 second)
- Smooth fade-in of the game environment
- Gentle floating particles in the background

### 4. **Meet Mira**
- See Mira's story introduction
- Learn that she can hear animal voices
- Discover that the wetland is in danger

### 5. **Make Your First Choice**
Select from 2-3 options:
- Each choice affects the story differently
- Your decisions impact the wetland's health
- Building friendship with animals matters

### 6. **Progress Through Story**
Over 8 chapters, you'll:
- Meet Kachua Ji (🐢), an old turtle
- Meet Finny (🐟), a young fish
- Face choices about plastic pollution, factory waste, and illegal development
- See real-time updates to the wetland health and mission status

### 7. **Track Your Progress**
Monitor on the right panel:
- 🌍 Wetland Health (0-100%) - Color changes: Green → Orange → Red
- 📋 Mission Status - Visual indicators for each task
- 💚 Friendship Level - Shows your bond with animals
- 🐾 Animal Conditions - Current status of Kachua Ji and Finny

### 8. **Reach Your Ending**
Based on all your choices, experience one of:
- ✨ **Good Ending**: Full restoration, happy animals, thriving wetland
- 🌱 **Balanced Ending**: Partial success, fragile progress, continued hope
- 💔 **Bad Ending**: Environmental lesson about consequences

### 9. **Replay for Different Outcomes**
Click "Play Again" to:
- Make different choices
- Explore alternative story paths
- Aim for the good ending

## Key Game Mechanics

### ❤️ Wetland Health
- Starts at 60%
- **Increases** when you:
  - Clean plastic pollution
  - Stop factory pollution
  - Prevent illegal development
  - Show compassion to animals

- **Decreases** when you:
  - Ignore animals' suffering
  - Delay action
  - Choose ineffective solutions

- **Status Messages**:
  - 85%+ = "✨ Wetland is thriving!"
  - 70%+ = "🌱 Wetland is recovering!"
  - 50%+ = "⚠️ Wetland is struggling..."
  - 30%+ = "💔 Wetland is in critical danger!"
  - <30% = "💀 Wetland is dying..."

### 💚 Friendship Level
- Starts at 0%
- **Increases** through:
  - Direct action to help animals
  - Listening and empathy
  - Community organizing
  - Compassionate choices

- **Affects**:
  - Which ending you get
  - How animals respond to you
  - Your access to certain solutions

### 📋 Mission Status
Track 3 key missions:
1. 🗑️ **Clean Plastic** - Address plastic pollution
2. 🏭 **Stop Pollution** - End factory pollution
3. 🏗️ **Prevent Development** - Stop illegal development

Each shows:
- ⚠️ **Pending** (Orange) - Not yet addressed
- ✅ **Completed** (Green) - Successfully resolved

## Decision Points & Consequences

### Chapter 1: Plastic Pollution
**Choice**: How to help Kachua Ji?
- 🧹 Clean immediately (+30 health, +40 friendship)
- 🤝 Talk and plan (+15 health, +30 friendship)
- 📱 Film and share (+10 health, +15 friendship)

**Impact**: Sets tone for your approach

### Chapter 2: Factory Pollution
**Choice**: How to address Finny's suffering?
- 🏭 Stop factory (-35 health impact, +45 friendship)
- 📞 Report to authorities (+25 health, +35 friendship)
- 🤝 Rally community (+25 health, +40 friendship)

**Impact**: Determines how pollution is resolved

### Chapter 3: Illegal Development
**Choice**: How to respond to machinery?
- 📞 Contact authorities (+35 health, +30 friendship)
- 🤝 Community protest (+30 health, +50 friendship)
- 📸 Document evidence (+15 health, +20 friendship)

**Impact**: Speed and effectiveness of response

### Chapter 4: Final Decision
**Choice**: How to resolve development conflict?
- 🛑 Force complete stop (+40 health, +45 friendship) → **Good Ending**
- 🤝 Negotiate compromise (+25 health, -5 friendship) → **Balanced Ending**
- 🚑 Evacuate animals (+5 health, -20 friendship) → **Bad Ending**

**Impact**: Determines which ending you receive
