### Core Gameplay ✅
- **8+ Story Chapters**: Progressive narrative
- **20+ Actions**: Different ways to respond to situations
- **7 Major Decisions**: Meaningful choice points
- **Dynamic Consequences**: All choices impact the outcome

### UI Components ✅
- **Character Dialogue Box**: Name, emoji, quotation marks, emotional text
- **Choice Buttons**: 2-3 options per scene with clear impact
- **Mission Status Panel**: Real-time tracking of 3 objectives
- **Wetland Health Bar**: Color-coded (green/orange/red) indicator
- **Friendship Level**: Shows relationship with animals
- **Animal Conditions**: Individual status for each character
- **Chapter Information**: Current story progression
- **Floating Particles**: Ambient background effect

### Character System ✅
- **Mira** (👧): Empathetic guide, can hear animal voices
- **Kachua Ji** (🐢): 40-year-old wise turtle with emotional depth
- **Finny** (🐟): Young fish struggling for survival

## 📖 STORY & DIALOGUE

### Emotional Narratives ✅
- **Plastic Pollution**: Kachua Ji tangled in waste, struggling to breathe
- **Factory Pollution**: Finny's water poisoned, siblings dying
- **Illegal Development**: Machinery destroying the wetland
- **Community Action**: The power of collective effort

### Dialogue Examples ✅

> "The water tastes bitter and burns my gills... many of my siblings didn't survive."

### Ending System ✅
- **Good Ending ✨**: Full restoration, thriving wildlife
- **Balanced Ending 🌱**: Partial success, fragile recovery
- **Bad Ending 💔**: Environmental lesson about consequences
